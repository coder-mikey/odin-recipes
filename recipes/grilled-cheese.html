Grilled Cheese

Information

Grilled Cheese is a classic American sandwich great as a snack. Serves 1.

Ingredients

  • thicker sliced bread such as texas toast
  • 1/2 Tbsp of butter on each slice of bread
  • sliced medium cheddar, gouda, harvati cheese

Steps

  1. spread 1/2 Tbsp butter on one side of each slice of bread
  2. place a skillet over low to medium/low heat. A griddle should be at 275˚F. Immediately add 2 slices of bread with the butter-side-down.
  3. stack cheeses on one of the pieces of bread, cover with the other piece of toast and flip the sandwich over.
  4. Continue sauteeing, flipping once, until both sides are golden brown and cheese is melted.
  5. cut the sandwich in half on the diagonal to serve
